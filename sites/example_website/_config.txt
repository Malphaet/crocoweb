Be wary that _ is a reserved character
Technically those lines are not comments but since the text content of config files is ignored, it acts like one

__en__

__/en__

__fr__
Cette partie est un commentaire français seulement
__/fr__

__comment__
You can also comment anywhere by using any
__lang__ not in the scope of generated ones
__/comment__
